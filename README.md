# Sales_Performance_Analysis:

Project Overview:
This project analyzes sales transaction data using Microsoft Excel to evaluate business performance across products, countries, customer segments, time periods, and discount levels.
The objective is to derive clear business insights and present them through structured analysis and a summary dashboard.

Dataset:
The dataset consists of transactional sales records with the following key fields: Order Date, Product, Country, Segment, Sales, Profit, Discount, Discount Band.

Data Cleaning & Preparation:
1. Checked for missing values and blank cells
2. Checked duplicate records
3.Standardized categorical fields (Product, Country, Segment)
4. Ensured correct data types for date and numeric columns
5. Verified consistency between Sales, Profit, and Discount
6. Created calculated fields such as Profit Margin

Analysis Performed:
1. Product-Level Analysis:
a. Calculated Total Sales and Total Profit by Product.
b. Identified: Top 3 products by Sales, Bottom 3 products by Profit.

2. Country-Level Analysis:
a. Analyzed Sales and Profit by Country.
b. Calculated Profit Margin to evaluate profitability across regions.

3. Segment Analysis:
a. Evaluated Sales and Profit by customer Segment.
b. Identified: Most profitable segment, Least profitable segment.

4. Time-Based Analysis:
a. Analyzed Sales trends by Year and Month.
b. Identified: Peak sales month, Seasonal sales patterns.

5. Discount Impact Analysis:
a. Compared Average Discount, Total Sales, and Total Profit by Discount Band.
b. Identified discount levels that negatively impact profitability.

6. Preapred auto upated KPIs sheet.

7. Dashboard: The dashboard is static and designed for clear presentation of insights rather than interactivity.

Key KPIs:
Total Sales, Total Profit, Overall Profit Margin, Best Performing Product (by Profit), Best Performing Country (by Profit).

Key Insights:

1. High sales volume does not always translate into high profitability.
2. Certain products generate strong revenue but low or negative profit.
3. Some countries contribute high sales with lower profit margins.
4. Clear seasonality is observed in monthly sales trends.
5. Higher discount bands increase sales volume but significantly reduce profitability.

Tools & Techniques Used:

1. Microsoft Excel
2. Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts
3. Calculated Fields
4. Basic KPI reporting
5. Conditional Formatting
